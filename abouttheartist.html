About the Artist

I have always been interested in art, but I gained a love for graphic design in high school. I enjoy illustrating on my iPad, working with Adobe Creative Suite and some forms of traditional art such as ink and watercolor. I plan to graduate from Iowa State, and along the way I hope to discover what career I would enjoy!

Educational Career

I have attended DMACC for classes in Web Development and Management part-time from 2018 to 2020 while working full time at Hobby Lobby in Ames, and finally landed on Graphic Design as my major. I was about a semester into the program at DMACC and loving it, and the opportunity came up to transfer to Iowa State University! I was so excited, and worked through the necessary process to transfer into their program. I am now a full-time Graphic Design student at Iowa State, and I plan to graduate in 2024.

Future Plans

I haven't quite decided which field of Graphic Design I want to work in yet. However, I am open to new opportunities and have previously thought about working with something like user experience design. I have also considered game design, although I am not expecting to start out working in that field.
